Why Computer Science Degree?

At a very young age my father bought me my very first computer where I was able to explore the amazing place the internet was becoming and soon change the way I saw the world. I still remember connecting to the internet through a dial-up connection and everything running very slow. I was involved with a very particular strategy game called StarCraft where I really began wondering how such a game could be developed with the help of a programming language. From then on I had already grown an interest in software with a little help from the math classes which I enjoyed very much. Before applying to colleges I already knew that a Computer Science degree would be the best choice for me. I can see now that the world is changing very fast and with the help of software design many more things are to come in the near future.
