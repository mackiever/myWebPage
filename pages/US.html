Growing up in the US

After moving to the U.S. I started going to my new school called Franklin School and later proceeded to go to Kearny High school. Kearny is and will always be my hometown and I have many wonderful memories growing up. While attending school I continued to have an interest in Math classes with the exeption of a few history classes. I was able to meet people from different parts of the world and got to know more about their culture. I have met people from Brazil, Mexico, Portugal, Poland, China, India, and other parts of Africa. It's been a true experience meeting so many different people from different parts of the world and I have seen how each of us play a significant role in our society. We work together in order to become a better person but most important find ways to work together as a team.
